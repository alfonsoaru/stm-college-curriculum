Units A & B - Scientific Foundations

Unit A - Biology

Cells and Body Systems

Introduction to the building blocks of life. Students explore cell structure, learn to use microscopes, and investigate how cells form tissues, organs, and organ systems.

Key Skills: Microscopy, cell identification, organ system mapping, scientific drawing

Unit B - Chemistry

Elements, Compounds & Mixtures

Understanding the fundamental building blocks of matter. Students learn about atoms, elements, and how they combine to form compounds and mixtures.

Key Skills: Element identification, compound formation, separation techniques, periodic table navigation

Units C & D - Building Understanding

Unit C - Physics

Forces and Motion

Exploring how forces affect the world around us. Students investigate different types of forces, their effects on objects, and how motion can be measured and predicted.

Key Skills: Force measurement, motion analysis, graph interpretation, experimental design

Unit D - Biology

Life Processes & Living Things

Understanding what makes something alive. Students explore the seven life processes and investigate how different organisms carry out these essential functions.

Key Skills: Life process identification, organism classification, habitat investigation, biodiversity assessment

Unit E - Chemistry

Chemical Reactions

Discovering how substances interact and change. Students observe chemical reactions, learn about reactants and products, and explore how reactions can be controlled.

Key Skills: Reaction observation, chemical equation writing, safety procedures, laboratory techniques

Unit F - Physics

Energy and Energy Transfers

Understanding energy in all its forms. Students explore different types of energy, how energy can be transferred and transformed, and the principle of energy conservation.

Key Skills: Energy identification, energy transfer mapping, efficiency calculations, renewable energy evaluation
