Geography Year 7 Q&A

How does Year 7 Geography build foundational skills?
Year 7 focuses on essential geographical skills including map reading, data collection, and spatial analysis. Students develop core competencies in using Ordnance Survey maps, understanding scale and grid references, and interpreting geographical data that will support all future geographical study.
What fieldwork opportunities are included?
Students participate in a single-use plastic fieldwork study, learning primary data collection techniques, hypothesis development, and data analysis. This hands-on experience develops practical geographical enquiry skills and environmental awareness.
How are global perspectives integrated?
The curriculum includes study of UK, European, and world geography, weather systems, ecosystems, and population patterns in Russia. Students develop understanding of global interconnections and diverse geographical environments.
What assessment methods are used?
Assessment includes practical map-making exercises, location knowledge tests, creative projects like rainforest animal diaries, fieldwork data analysis, and case study examinations of weather hazards and demographic patterns.
