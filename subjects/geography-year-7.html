Year 7 Geography Journey

Year 7 Geography at STM College introduces students to essential geographical skills and understanding of our world. Beginning with foundational map skills and progressing through weather systems, ecosystems, and global population patterns, students develop the ability to think geographically about places, environments, and human interactions with the natural world.
