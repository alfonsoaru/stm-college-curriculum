Year 7 RE Journey

Year 7 Religious Education at STM College provides a comprehensive introduction to Catholic faith and practice while fostering respectful understanding of diverse religious traditions. Students begin their journey exploring Creation and Covenant, progress through the life and teachings of Jesus Christ, discover the power of the Holy Spirit, and conclude with meaningful inter-faith dialogue through the study of Hinduism. This curriculum develops critical thinking, moral reasoning, and spiritual reflection skills essential for thoughtful engagement with faith and ethics.
