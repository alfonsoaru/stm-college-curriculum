🍂 Autumn Term

Terms 1a & 1b - Personal Introduction & School Life

Term 1a - La rentrée

Getting to Know You

Introducing yourself in French including name, age, family members, opinions, classroom objects, and describing personality traits. Building confidence in basic conversational French.

Key Grammar: Present tense of -er verbs, adjective agreement, infinitive forms

Term 1b - En classe

School & Learning

Exploring school life through colours, telling time, school subjects, uniforms, likes and dislikes. Understanding the French education system and daily school routines.

Key Grammar: Colour adjective agreement, -er verb conjugation, sequencing words

Terms 2a & 2b - Hobbies & Family Life

Term 2a - Mon temps libre

Free Time & Sports

Discussing weather, seasons, sports activities, and personal preferences. Learning about sport in French-speaking countries and creating celebrity interviews.

Key Grammar: Jouer and faire conjugation, negatives, adjective position, infinitives, aimer/adorer/détester

Language Skills Development

Year 7 French develops essential language skills through engaging topics and systematic grammar progression, building confidence for future language learning.

🗣️

Speaking & Pronunciation

Building confidence in spoken French through role-plays, interviews, and authentic communication activities.

👂

Listening Comprehension

Understanding authentic French through varied listening activities, developing skills to comprehend different accents and speeds.

📖

Reading Skills

Reading and understanding a range of French texts from simple descriptions to cultural information about French-speaking countries.

✍️

Writing Communication

Developing writing skills from basic descriptions to creative tasks, focusing on accuracy and personal expression.

🏛️

Cultural Awareness

Exploring French and Francophone cultures through festivals, traditions, school systems, and daily life comparisons.

⚙️

Grammar Foundation

Building essential grammar knowledge systematically through present tense verbs, adjectives, and basic sentence structures.

Key Vocabulary Themes

Year 7 French vocabulary is organized around practical themes that connect to students' everyday experiences and interests:

Personal Information

Frères et sœurs (brothers and sisters), Ma salle de classe (my classroom), Tu es comment? (What are you like?), Tu aimes ça? (Do you like that?)

School Life

Quelle heure (what time), Les matières (subjects), Les vêtements (clothes), Journée scolaire (school day)

Activities & Weather

Le temps (weather), Les sports (sports), Les opinions (opinions), Les pays (countries)

Family & Home

La famille (family), Où habites-tu? (Where do you live?), Le petit déjeuner (breakfast)

Town & Social Life

Le weekend (weekend), Les invitations (invitations), Vous désirez (what would you like), Le verbe aller (verb to go)

Language Tools

Opinions, Linking words, Descriptions, Reasons, Adjectives, Tenses

Assessment & Progress Tracking

Year 7 French assessment covers all four language skills through module-based testing, ongoing speaking assessment, and formal end-of-year examinations. Students progress through reading comprehension, listening tasks, and communication-focused writing activities.

Cultural Connections

Year 7 French learning is enriched through cultural exploration and real-world connections:

French Education System

Comparing French and UK schools, understanding different educational approaches and daily routines in French schools.

Francophone Countries

Exploring French-speaking countries and their cultures, particularly through sports and leisure activities.

French Celebrations

Learning about Bastille Day and other French cultural celebrations, understanding their historical and social significance.

Practical Communication

Developing real-world language skills for traveling, ordering food, and interacting in French-speaking environments.
