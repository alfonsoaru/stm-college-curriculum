PE Year 7 Q&A

How are students assessed in PE?
Students complete assessment booklets every 6th lesson, focusing on skill development, technique improvement, and understanding of rules and tactics. This includes baseline fitness tests and ongoing practical performance evaluation.
What sports and activities are covered in Year 7?
Year 7 covers volleyball, trampolining, table tennis, badminton, gymnastics, athletics, cricket, rounders, and boxing fundamentals. Students experience game, net/wall, strike/field, and gymnastic activities.
How does PE support fitness and health education?
The curriculum includes Health-Related Fitness (HRF) components covering ROPHA principles (Rate, Overload, Progression, Health, Adherence), circuit training, and understanding of cardiovascular fitness and body conditioning.
What safety measures are in place?
Safety is paramount with structured progressions in activities like trampolining and boxing. Students learn proper techniques, safety protocols, and risk assessment while developing physical competence and confidence.
