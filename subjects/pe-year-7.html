Year 7 Physical Education Journey

Year 7 PE at STM College provides a comprehensive introduction to physical education through diverse sporting activities. Students develop fundamental movement skills across invasion games, net and wall games, strike and field activities, gymnastics, and athletics. The curriculum emphasizes skill development, tactical awareness, health-related fitness, and building confidence in physical activity through carefully structured progressions that ensure every student experiences success and personal improvement.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What sports and activities will students experience in Year 7 PE?
A: Year 7 students experience a wide variety of activities including football, basketball, volleyball, badminton, table tennis, gymnastics, trampolining, athletics, cricket, rounders, and introduction to boxing techniques. This diverse curriculum ensures students develop a broad range of physical skills and discover activities they enjoy.
Q: What PE kit is required for Year 7?
A: Students need STM PE polo shirt, black shorts or sports leggings, trainers for indoor activities, football boots or astro trainers for outdoor games, and a warm tracksuit for outdoor lessons in colder weather. All items should be clearly labeled with the student's name.
Q: How are students assessed in PE?
A: Students are assessed through practical performance in activities, completion of assessment booklets every sixth lesson, baseline fitness testing, and demonstration of skill development over time. Assessment focuses on effort, improvement, technique, tactical understanding, and application of skills in competitive situations.
Q: What happens if my child forgets their PE kit?
A: Students who forget PE kit will be provided with spare kit to change into. Regular kit forgetting may result in sanctions. Students should always participate in PE lessons as physical education is a vital part of their overall education and wellbeing.
Q: How does the PE department support students who lack confidence in physical activities?
A: We differentiate activities to ensure success for all students, provide positive encouragement, use peer support systems, and offer additional support through extra-curricular clubs. Every lesson is designed with progressive skill levels so all students can experience achievement and build confidence gradually.
